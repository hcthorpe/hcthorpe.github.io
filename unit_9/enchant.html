The Enchantments

This dime piece of a place is located in Washington State. It's so beautiful, that if you want to camp in The Enchantments, you must enter the lottery at recreation.gov. Otherwise, you can only day hike it but this place deserves to be savored for a few days. There are three options for camping: Colchuk Lake, The Core or Snow Lake. All are divine but it's like you won a million dollars if you get The Core and half a milly is you get the other two. Husein's lucky charm won us a 4 day spot at Snow Lake.
