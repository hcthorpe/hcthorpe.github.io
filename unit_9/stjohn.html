St. John, USVI

I'm one of *those* people who thoroughly enjoys celebrating birthdays. For me, it’s a time that compels reflection, optimism and gratitude.

As my 30th birthday approached, we knew we’d have to plan a legendary adventure to kick off this new decade I was entering. My birthday is in December, and here in Virginia, the weather can be touch-and-go. I love warm weather and camping, so a simple Google search of “camping in the Caribbean” settled it, St. John in the US Virgin Islands it is.

In just 20 square miles, St. John is home to the only National Park in the Caribbean and some of the most beautiful beaches imaginable. It’s a mountain meets beach utopia.
